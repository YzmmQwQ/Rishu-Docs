# Having Issues?

Before proceeding, please make sure you've read "[How To Ask Questions](/en/learn-more/how-to-ask)" and have already checked the common issues.

## Common Issues

### "Account at Risk" or "Abnormal Status" When Adding Friend

This is a default reaction from QQ's risk control system and doesn't necessarily mean there's an actual problem. See "[About Messaging Platform Issues](/en/before-adding/platform-issues)" for details.

### Bot Not Responding

Possible causes:

1. **Bot is offline (indicated by missing "status" under the bot name)** — The bot may be offline for maintenance or blocked by risk control, wait and try again
2. **Bot chose not to reply** — The bot decided not to respond to your message
3. **Model request error** — The model encountered an error, wait for maintainers to fix it
4. **Profile page shows "Abnormal Account Status"** — The bot is affected by risk control

### Feature Not Working or Errors

1. Confirm your command format is correct
2. Check if you have permission to use that feature
3. Bot commands may conflict with built-in LLM features (rare)

## Self-Check Steps

Before reporting an issue, please follow these steps:

1. **Confirm the issue exists** — This might be intermittent, wait and try several times
2. **Check your network** — Ensure your network environment and client are working properly
3. **Check announcements** — Check QQ Space for maintenance/outage notices

## How to Report Issues

If the problem persists after self-checking, please report it:

### Information to Provide

- **Problem description**: What happened? What was expected?
- **Steps to reproduce**: How did you trigger the problem?
- **Screenshots/Logs**: Provide complete error screenshots or logs

### Channels

- DM Rishu to send feedback (ignore the LLM replies for now)

### When Reporting

- Be polite, don't rush
- Provide complete information, don't just say "it doesn't work"
- Remember to thank those who helped after the issue is resolved
